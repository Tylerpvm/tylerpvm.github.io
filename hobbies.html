Hobbies

Hockey

Here we go agian with Colorado... It's no different with Colorado sports, I've been a Broncos fan for as long as I can remember. However, my true passion is Hockey! My wife and I are die hard Avalanche fans. We have hardly missed any of the 82 games in the regular season! We went to the Blues/Avs playoff game 2 years ago with some good friends and the Avs won that game, then went on to win the Stanley Cup!

Photography

Another one of our passions is travelling. We have been on so many adventures in the 9 years that we have been together. We have been as far NorthWest as Rialto Beach in Washington State, and as far SouthEast as southern Florida. We have an Alaskan cruise planned for later this year and that will be the first time that we have been to Alaska.

Going on these adventures has taught us something, we love photography. We both have camera gear and we go wild, everywhere we go. It doesn't matter if its just a random hike 10 miles away, or if it's travelling thousands of miles away, we always have our camera gear! We are no experts by any means but that doesn't stop us from loving it! I have some of our photos listed below that came from a road trip we took a few years ago. That trip took us 3500 miles and we hit 11 states, including 4 national parks!
