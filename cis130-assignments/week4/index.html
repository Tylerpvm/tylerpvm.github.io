Rocky Mountain National Park

Welcome!

Rocky Mountain National Park is a must for any hiking, camping, or adventuring enthusiast! Home to 415 square miles of meadows, alpine lakes, and towering mountain peaks!

Explore

Rocky Mountain National Park has 355 miles of hiking trails. At just over 2.6 miles hikers will reach the foot of Mills Lake, nestled just below Half Mountain. If you're looking for a more casual hike, you might prefer the Bear Lake trail. At just 0.5 miles, you can loop around bear lake and takes an average of just 11 minutes!

Learn

Explore the history and nature of the Rocky Mountains on a ranger-led program. Walks, talks, and other ranger-guided activities are free and open to the public.

Plan Ahead

Keep an eye on the weather! The rocky mountains are unforgiving when it comes to sudden storms! Shaped by elevation, slope, and exposure, these patterns can change rapidly. Always be prepared for varying weather - conditions can change rapidly.
